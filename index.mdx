spark is an intelligence platform for teams that work with AI agents.

your team generates a mountain of signal every day. commits, PRs, deploys, slack messages, asana tickets, agent sessions. none of it connects. it sits in silos. nobody synthesizes it. ask "what happened this week?" and someone has to manually stitch it together from five different tools.

spark fixes that. it ingests your team's work, scores it, lets you ask questions about it, and builds a compounding knowledge base that gets smarter every day.

## the four pillars

spark is built on four connected layers. each one builds on the one before it.

## who spark is for

spark is for teams that work with AI agents, automated pipelines, and complex toolchains. teams that want to actually understand what's happening across all of it.

engineering teams. devops. AI-native startups. any team where work moves faster than humans can track it.
